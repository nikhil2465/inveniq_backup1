Design Quote Studio

Built for interior designers and architects.

A dedicated, restricted portal where architects log in and see only their Design Quote Studio — no inventory noise, no finance data. Just their pipeline.

  • Room-by-room interior quotations with AI-assisted BOQ — kitchen, bedroom, living room, bathroom
  • WhatsApp brief scanner — photograph client brief, AI extracts rooms & dimensions
  • Area calculator — dimensions → auto-compute sq.ft for each room
  • Architect Fee Proposals — 5–8% fee on project value, split across P1–P6 phases
  • 6-phase milestone billing — Concept (P1), Schematic (P2), Design Dev (P3), Working Drawings (P4), Tender (P5), Site Supervision (P6)
  • GST compliant — SAC code 998331 · 18% design service tax
  • Quote statuses — DRAFT → SENT → NEGOTIATING → WON / LOST / EXPIRED
  • AI context — GPT-4o knows fee benchmarks, material rates, and Indian market context
