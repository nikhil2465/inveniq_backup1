## RECORDING TIPS
- Speak at 80% of your normal speed — it always sounds faster on playback
- Pause 1 second before and after clicking anything important
- If you make a mistake, pause, take a breath, and continue — easy to cut in editing
- For the AI chat segments, pre-type the questions before the take so you're not hunting for words
- The best takes are when you sound curious about your own product — not reading a script
